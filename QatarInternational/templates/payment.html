Send Money via bKash

  1. Step 1: Open your bKash App or dial *247#

    On your smartphone, open the bKash app. If you don't have the app, you can also use the USSD code by dialing *247#.

  2. Step 2: Select "Send Money"

    In the bKash app, tap on the "Send Money" option. If you are using USSD, reply with the corresponding number for "Send Money".

  3. Step 3: Enter the bKash Receiver Number

  4. Step 4: Enter the Amount

  5. Step 5: Enter Reference (Optional)

  6. Step 6: Enter your bKash PIN

  7. Step 7: Confirm the Transaction

    Review the details (receiver number, amount) and confirm the transaction by tapping "Send" in the app or by replying with "1" in USSD.

  8. Step 8: Transaction Successful

    You will receive a confirmation message from bKash regarding the successful transaction.

Scan bKash QR Code to Send Money (If Applicable)

If the receiver has provided a bKash QR code, you can scan it using the bKash app:

  1. Step 1: Open your bKash App.
  2. Step 2: Tap on the "Scan QR" option.
  3. Step 3: Scan the QR code below.
  4. Step 4: Follow steps 4-7 above (enter amount, reference, PIN, and confirm).
